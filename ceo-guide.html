RelayNorth CEO Control Guide

Lead direction.
Let the agents work.

This private guide explains what is running, where you step in, and what must never happen without human approval.

Start Here

Open http://localhost:8501. The dashboard is the command room: revenue, agents, approvals, inbox review, experiments, and backlog.

Your Role

You are CEO. You set direction, approve risky actions, and unblock decisions. The system drafts, monitors, files, researches, and queues.

Never Automate

Refunds, discounts, spending, live posting, legal terms, bank details, production publishing, or live customer sends without approval.

Daily Review

  1. Check Growth OS approvals.
  2. Review inbox/commercial safeguard items.
  3. Approve or reject outreach/marketing drafts.
  4. Check if revenue or replies moved.
  5. Pick one next decision.

Agent Teams

Coordinator, Outreach, Comms Monitor, Solution Builder, Payments, Training, Marketing, Forum, Market Research, Experimentation, Backlog Research.

Run Commands

runtime/.venv/bin/python runtime/scripts/autonomous/control.py start

runtime/.venv/bin/python runtime/scripts/autonomous/growth_os.py --agent all --dry-run

runtime/.venv/bin/python runtime/scripts/autonomous/growth_os.py --status

When To Intervene

Intervene when the dashboard says: needs approval, blocked, nothing moving, automation failed, opportunity at risk, or commercial/security review required.

Weekly Rhythm

Review reply rate, paid audits, competitor research, experiments, and backlog. Kill one weak thing. Approve one useful test.
